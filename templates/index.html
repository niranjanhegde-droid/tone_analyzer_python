How the engine works

Two independent models, combined, so one model's blind spot never dominates the verdict.

🧠

Trained NB Classifier

A Multinomial Naive Bayes model, trained on 180+ labeled meeting utterances via TF-IDF, predicts positive / neutral / negative / aggressive / anxious tone.

📖

Sentiment Lexicon

A hand-built VADER-style lexicon scores valence with negation handling and intensifiers, catching nuance the classifier alone can miss.

📊

Meeting Health Score

Filler words, politeness markers, hedging, and aggression cues combine into a single 0–100 meeting health index per transcript.

🗣️

Per-Speaker Breakdown

Paste a "Speaker: text" transcript and get individual tone profiles — useful for spotting who is driving tension in a call.

🔒

Private by Default

Accounts, transcripts, and reports are stored per-user in a local SQLite database — nothing leaves your deployment.

⚡

Instant Results

The whole pipeline runs in-process with no external AI API calls needed — analysis returns in well under a second.
